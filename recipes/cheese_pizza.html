Cheese Pizza

Description

Cheese Pizza: A simple dish that is one of life's greatest pleasures. However, you sometimes wonder if you should make your own pizza instead of constantly calling for delivery. Fret not! This recipe turns great into grandiose with the addition of exceptional pizza dough, low-moisture mozzarella cheese, and an easy to make tomato sauce. With a final, simple garnish of fresh herbs, you'll soon find yourself in pizza heaven.

Ingredients

  • ¼ ounce of dry yeast
  • 1 cup of lukewarm water
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 3 cups of bread flour
  • ½ teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • ½ cup of tomato puree
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 minced garlic clove
  • 1 tablespoon of chopped fresh basil
  • 4 ounces of low-moisture mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil or oregano leaves, for garnishing

Steps

  1. Mix yeast, water, and sugar in a glass or plastic bowl.
  2. Stir to dissolve the yeast and let the yeast "bloom" for 15 minutes.
  3. Stir in 1 cup of flour, add salt, and then stir in another cup of flour.
  4. Dump the mixture onto the kneading board and add in the last cup of flour.
  5. Keep kneading until the dough is soft and elastic, and form the dough into a ball.
  6. In another bowl, pour in 1 tablespoon of olive oil and spread it around the bowl.
  7. Coat the ball of dough with the oil and cover the bowl with a damp towel.
  8. Let the dough rise for 40 minutes.
  9. Punch down the dough and knead on the board for about 2 minutes.
  10. To ensure that the crust won't stick, lightly spray the pizza pan with olive oil or vegetable oil spray and then work the dough to the pan.
  11. To make the tomato sauce, mix together the pureed tomatoes, tomato paste, minced garlic, and basil.
  12. Spread the sauce onto the prepared pizza dough.
  13. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese and bake at 500°F for 11-13 minutes.
  14. Garnish with fresh basil or oregano, if desired, and cut into wedges.
  15. Enjoy your homemade pizza!
