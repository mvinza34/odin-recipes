Cheese Pizza

Description

Cheese Pizza: A simple dish that is one of life's greatest pleasures. However, you sometimes wonder if you should make your own pizza instead of constantly calling for delivery. Fret not! This recipe turns great into grandiose with the addition of exceptional pizza dough, low-moisture mozzarella cheese, and an easy to make tomato sauce. With a final, simple garnish of fresh herbs, you'll soon find yourself in pizza heaven.
