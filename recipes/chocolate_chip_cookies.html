Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

When it comes to desserts, chocolate chip cookies are viewed as one of the best out there, and for good reason. Crisp edges and chewy middles loaded with chocolate chips are what to many people make chocolate chip cookies absolutely irresistible. This recipe is here to help people make said cookies, and will quickly become your go-to.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of softened butter
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1 cup of packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons of hot water
  • ½ of teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Soften the butter, and make sure the eggs are at room temperature.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Beat the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.
  3. Add and beat the eggs one at a time, and stir in the vanilla.
  4. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water. Add it to the batter along with salt.
  5. Stir in the flour and chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven until edges are nicely browned for about 10 minutes.
  8. Let the cookies cool for about 3-5 minutes.
  9. Store the cookies in a container or serve them and enjoy!
